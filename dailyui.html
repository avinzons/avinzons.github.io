What is good visual design?

After taking CU App's Dev's course in Intro to Product Design, I began to see design as problem solving rather than appealing aesthetics and visuals. However, every product I looked at seemed to excel exceedingly at both. Since I was never one of the art-school-dreaming kids, I was concerned about my lack of talent in the latter, I decided to devote more time to browsing the online work of the design community on sites like behance and dribbble. I picked up the DailyUI challenge to push content, knowing that the only way to improve is to practice.
