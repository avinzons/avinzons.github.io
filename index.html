I study deep learning methods for natural language processing (NLP), and computational sociology. Particularly, I focus on developing a system that extracts contexual meaning of human language by interacting with external knowledge. My goal is to develop multi-agents systems that can communicate and actively learn from the environment

Currently I work with Prof. Michael Macy (Cornell) to identify implicit bias in information diffustion, and Prof. Yejin Choi (University of Wachington) to develop neural style transfer for gender-neutral sentences
